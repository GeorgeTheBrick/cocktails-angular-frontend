Welcome to our Cocktail Application!

A simple app, where you can find all your favourite cocktails

With this app, you can:

  • Search cocktails by name, ingredient or ID
  • Sort your list of cocktails in ascending/descending order
  • Filter to only show alcoholic or non-alcoholic cocktails
  • Generate a random cocktail, which is also dependent on your filter

To start, scroll the list and Select a Cocktail!
